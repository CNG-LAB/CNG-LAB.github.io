Our research seeks to understand the foundations of brain network organization in integrative and higher-order cognition. We develop various interdisciplinary approaches that integrate neuroimaging and genetics with large-scale network models and non-brain phenotypes. Our multi-level research rests on three complementary themes that address the interplay of structural wiring, functional interactions, and cognition in healthy and diseased brains.
